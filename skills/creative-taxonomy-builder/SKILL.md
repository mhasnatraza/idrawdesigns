# Creative Taxonomy Builder

## Workflow
1. Create top-level discipline groups.
2. Add subcategories for style, medium, era, and use-case.
3. Add marketing intent tags (awareness, consideration, conversion, retention).
4. Add production tags (image, video, static, motion, print, social).
5. Version taxonomy updates in a changelog.

## Required discipline groups
- Fine art and art movements
- Graphic design and typography
- Branding and advertising
- Photography and cinematography
- Illustration and sketching
- Sculpture and installation
- Handicrafts and DIY
- Performance arts
- Interior and spatial design
- Fashion design

## Guardrails
- Keep tags mutually understandable and reusable.
- Avoid adding duplicate synonyms without aliases.
- Always preserve backward compatibility for existing entries.
